Security & Proctoring Requirements

This exam is proctored to ensure integrity. The following monitoring will be active during your assessment.
Webcam Monitoring
Your webcam will capture your image throughout the exam. Ensure your face is clearly visible at all times.
Screen Recording
Your screen activity will be recorded. Do not switch tabs or open other applications during the exam.
Audio Monitoring
Audio will be recorded to detect any external assistance or communication.
Prohibited Activities
  • Switching browser tabs or opening other applications
  • Using external resources, notes, or assistance
  • Communicating with others during the exam
  • Leaving the camera frame or covering your webcam
